How the 2nd & 4th Saturday Rule Works

Since 2015, the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and other financial institutions have standardized Saturday holidays. This rule helps streamline operations for public and private sector banks. However, if a month has five Saturdays, banks will be open on the 5th Saturday.
